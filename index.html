About the Poet

Swarnim Suhane is a dreamer of verses, storyteller of silences, and the soul behind Hansila. Through words, he captures the delicate echoes of love, loss, time, and self-reflection. Hansila is not just poetry — it’s a journey through emotions etched on a digital scroll.

Ek Lamha Ek Yaad – The Full Collection

EK PAIGAM – Lamha 1: Jazbaat ka Paighaam

Jazbaat ka paighaam tha, lafzon se bhej diya, Ek ehsaas tha dil ka, kagaz pe likh diya. Har harf mein tha dard, har she’r mein tha pyaar, Kya tum samajh paaye, mere jazbaat ka izhaar?

CHAAR BAATEIN 2 YAADEIN

Char baatein thi tumse kehni, do yaadein thi tumhari, Ek lamha tha jo tha khaas, aur ek tasveer purani. Waqt se guzar gaye hum, yaadon ke saaye mein, Par dil ab bhi ruk jaata hai, uss choti si mulaqat mein.

GHAR EK YAAD

Ghar tha woh, jisme deewar nahi, bas teri khushboo thi, Ek aangan tha, jahan har subah teri muskurahat gungunati thi. Ab toh bas ek yaad hai woh ghar, Jo pal mein jeeta tha aur hamesha ke liye chhup gaya andar.

KOYAL

Koyal jaise bolti thi, tu har mod pe zindagi, Teri awaaz mein bas gaya tha mere geet ka sur. Ab woh bolti nahi, par har subah, Main uski yaadon ka geet phir se likhta hoon.

ASHQ

Ek ashq tha, jo baahon mein tham gaya, Ek dard tha, jo muskurahat ban gaya. Zindagi ne kya-kya nahi diya, Par sabse gehri thi woh aansuon ki khamoshi.

TASSAVUR KI TANAHAI

Tassavur mein aayi thi tu, ek khwab si lagti thi, Tanhaai mein bhi ek saathi thi tu, yaadon mein basti thi. Ab bhi jab akela hota hoon, Toh teri muskurahat ki tasveer saamne aati hai.

ZINDAGI EK PARINDA

Zindagi ek parinda hai, jo udta hai aasman se umeed ke, Kabhi girta hai, kabhi sambhalta hai, par rukta nahi. Aur main? Main bas us parinde ka safar hoon, Jo har baar naye aasman ki talaash mein nikalta hai.
